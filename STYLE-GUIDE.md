# Ember & Oak — Style Guide

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Ember & Oak
- **Tagline**: Wood-Fired Flavors, Warm Hospitality
- **Tone**: Warm, inviting, confident. Elegant but approachable.

## Colors
- **Primary Dark**: #1a1a1a (deep charcoal backgrounds)
- **Warm Gold**: #D4A853 (accents, CTAs, highlights)
- **Cream**: #F5F0E8 (text on dark, light backgrounds)
- **Rich Brown**: #8B6F47 (secondary accents)
- **Deep Red**: #8B2635 (wine/accent color)
- **Text Light**: #E8E0D4 (body text on dark)
- **Text Dark**: #2C2C2C (body text on light)
- **Card Background**: #242424 (dark mode cards)

## Typography
- **Heading Font**: Playfair Display (serif, elegant)
- **Body Font**: Inter or system sans-serif
- **Menu Items**: 18px, semi-bold
- **Prices**: Playfair Display, gold color

## Design Principles
- Dark by default — restaurants look best in moody lighting
- Food imagery is EVERYTHING (CSS gradient placeholders for now)
- Generous white space — let the content breathe
- Gold accents sparingly — they lose impact if overused
- Rounded corners subtle (8px max) — not bubbly
- Mobile-first: menu, reservation, hours — that's what phone users want
