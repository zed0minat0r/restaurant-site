# Ember & Oak — Restaurant Website Vision

## Purpose
Professional website for an upscale-casual restaurant that makes people hungry, showcases the menu, and drives reservations.

## Target Audience
- Local diners looking for a great dinner spot
- Couples planning date nights
- Groups booking for celebrations
- Foodies checking out the menu before visiting
- People searching "restaurants near me"

## What This Site Should Convey
- Warm, inviting atmosphere — make them FEEL the restaurant before visiting
- Quality food with beautiful presentation
- Professional but not pretentious — upscale-casual, not fine dining
- Easy to book a table — reservation should be 2 taps
- Clear hours, location, and contact info

## Design Direction
- Dark, warm color palette (deep charcoal, warm amber/gold, cream accents)
- Food-forward — the menu and dishes should be the hero
- Elegant typography — serif for headings, clean sans for body
- Moody, appetizing aesthetic — think candlelit dinner vibes
- Mobile-first — most people search for restaurants on their phones
