Our Story

Born from Flame

What started as a backyard obsession with live-fire cooking became something bigger. In 2019, Chef Marcus Rivera and sommelier Elena Torres opened the doors to Ember & Oak with a simple belief: the best meals happen around a fire.

Every dish on our menu is touched by flame — whether it's oak-smoked, charred, or slow-roasted over embers. We source seasonal ingredients from local farms within 50 miles, because great cooking starts with great ingredients.

The space itself is designed to feel like a gathering — warm woods, soft lighting, an open kitchen where you can watch the fire work. Whether you're here for a quiet dinner for two or a celebration with twenty, our goal is the same: make you feel at home, then blow you away with the food.
